Cacio e Pepe

Today we're going to be making 'Cacio e Pepe', the Italy's mac-n-cheese. The cheesy pasta puts a smile on your face, while the Saison adds so much depth you'll consider auditioning for Masterchef. This spectacular recipe is targeted for your begginner chef and struggling college student alike.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Saison beer
  • Pecorino romano cheese
  • Coarse black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Salt the water throughly before adding your pasta.
  3. Boil your pasta to 'just' under the package instructions, make sure to reserve some of the pasta water.
  4. Melt your butter down in a pan.
  5. Toast your pepper in the butter until fragrant.
  6. Add your Saison to the mixture.
  7. Allow to combine before adding your pasta and a little of the water.
  8. Take the pan off the heat and toss the pasta with your cheese.
  9. Enjoy.
