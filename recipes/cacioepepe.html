Cacio e Pepe

Today we're going to be making 'Cacio e Pepe', the Italy's mac-n-cheese. The cheesy pasta puts a smile on your face, while the Saison adds so much depth you'll consider auditioning for Masterchef. This spectacular recipe is targeted for your begginner chef and struggling college student alike.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Saison beer
  • Pecorino romano cheese
  • Coarse black pepper
