Hashbrowns

Today we're going to be making 'Hashbrowns'. Good old fashioned restaurant-style hash browns. Perfect with hot pepper sauce and ketchup!

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then drain and squeeze dry.
  2. Place shreds in a bowl, and mix in the onion
  3. Heat about 1/4 inch of oil in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. When oil is sizzling hot, place potatoes into the pan in a 1/2 inch thick layer.
  5. Cook until nicely browned on the bottom, then flip over and brown on the other side.
  6. Remove from pan, and drain on paper towels. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
  7. Enjoy.
